Sales Dashboard Project
 Project Overview
This project was built using Power BI to provide a clear overview of sales and operating profit.
The dashboard highlights key performance indicators (KPIs), monthly sales trends, and breakdowns by region, state, product, and retailer.

Analysis
* Total Sales: 899.90M
* Operating Profit: 332.13M
* Price per Unit: 436.25K
* Monthly Sales Trend: Sales grow steadily through spring and summer, drop slightly in autumn, then peak again in December.
* Regional Sales:
o West: 30%
o Southeast: 20.7%
o South: 18.1%
o Northeast: 16%
o Midwest: 15%
* Product Performance: Men’s apparel and athletic footwear lead in sales.
* Retailers: West Gear and Foot Locker are the top contributors.

Key Findings
* The West region is the strongest in sales performance.
* Men’s apparel is the highest-selling category.
* Major retailers like West Gear and Foot Locker drive a large share of revenue.
* Clear seasonality: sales increase in summer and December.

Tools Used
* Power BI Desktop
* Excel / CSV (data source)
* Power Query (data cleaning & preparation)
* DAX (for KPIs and calculations)

 Recommendations
1. Focus investment on the West region due to its high performance.
2. Strengthen marketing around Men’s apparel to maximize returns.
3. Build stronger partnerships with major retailers like West Gear and Foot Locker.
4. Launch seasonal promotions during autumn to offset lower sales.
5. Monitor Women’s apparel performance and adjust strategy to boost demand.
